About Me

A student who likes to create.

My name is Joven, and I'm a student from the Philippines. When I'm not busy with school, I split my free time between two hobbies: producing music and coding.

I use FL Studio to make beats and compose tracks. Separately, I enjoy building small websites and apps to learn new things. This website is just a simple place for me to share some of the things I've made.
