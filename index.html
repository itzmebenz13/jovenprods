About Me

A creative mind exploring the digital world.

Hi, I'm Joven! I'm a passionate student from the Philippines who finds joy in the intersection of technology and creativity. My journey spans across web development and music production, two worlds that might seem different but share the same creative essence.

When I'm not immersed in coursework, you'll find me crafting beats in FL Studio or building digital experiences with code. Each project is an opportunity to learn something new and push the boundaries of what I can create.

This portfolio is more than just a showcase—it's a reflection of my growth as a creator and my commitment to continuous learning in both music and technology.
